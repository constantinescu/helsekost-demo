About Our Klinik

Welcome to Natural Healing Rehabilitation Clinic, where we combine modern rehabilitation science with the healing power of medicinal plants and acupuncture. Our holistic approach treats the whole person, not just symptoms.


Founded on the principle that true healing embraces both conventional and traditional methods, our clinic offers a unique path to recovery and wellness that harnesses the best of both worlds.


Our specialists work together to create personalized treatment plans that may include physical rehabilitation, medicinal plant therapies, acupuncture, and nutritional guidance—all designed to help you achieve optimal health naturally.

Our Specialists

Hongling Mo Sønnichsen

Master i Rehabilitering & Health Consultant

Hongling Mo Sønnichsen is a certified practitioner in both acupuncture and zoneterapi, disciplines she has practiced and refined for many years and she brings a unique blend of scientific knowledge and holistic healing practices to her clients.


Her acupuncture treatments are rooted in Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) and are used to address various physical and emotional conditions, including chronic pain, stress, fatigue, digestive disorders, and hormonal imbalances. Her reflexology sessions focus on stimulating pressure points on the feet to activate the body's natural healing processes, aiming to restore balance and promote overall well-being.


Combining her scientific background with holistic therapies, Hongling adopts an integrative approach to health and rehabilitation. Her clients appreciate her attentive and personalized care, which is tailored to support recovery and enhance quality of life.

Dr. Torben Sønnichsen

Health Consultant & Biologist

Dr. Torben Sønnichsen is a seasoned biologist and health consultant with over three decades of experience in integrative health and natural rehabilitation. Holding a Ph.D. in Biology from the University of Copenhagen, his academic journey ignited a profound interest in medicinal herbs and their effects on human health.


As the founder of Øbro Helsekost Rehabilitering Klinik, Dr. Sønnichsen is promoting holistic health approaches that combine scientific knowledge with natural therapies.


Beyond his clinical practice, Dr. Sønnichsen is an advocate for sustainable health solutions. He has authored the book Den Sunde Aldring med Helsekost, which offers insights into healthy aging through diet, supplements, and lifestyle choices. The book also discusses regulatory challenges in Denmark's health supplement industry and proposes reforms to align with broader European standards.
